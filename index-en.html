How we work

COMPANY MAXWOOD

Manufactures and builds wooden houses from quality forests of Russia and Ukraine. Our team of experienced professionals will help you to choose and make a wooden house on the selected or individual project. Our houses are in demand in Europe for their Ecology, speed of installation and natural design.

Our works

A total of Four Steps:

Project

We develop a project based on your wishes and technical capabilities.

Contract

We agree on the budget, we sign the contract.

Blockhouse

Cut the log cabin at our site, disassemble for transportation.

House

We collect the log house from you and perform additional work.
